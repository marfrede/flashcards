HOW TO USE "FLASHCARDS"

Home:
- Create a set and give it a name plus description via the "(+) ADD SET" button. If you choose the "Private" option your set will not be displayed to other users in the "GLOBAL" section and it will be marked with a little red icon.
- Add cards after you created your set by entering your text into the inputfields "front and "back" at the top of the page.
- As long as the set exists you are able to make changes. (delete/edit/removing or adding cards)
- You can already see the cards that you added to your set beneath the "ADD FLASHCARD" button. Here you can also search for or sort the cards of your set
- on a flashcard: Swipe to the left and press the appearing red button to delete it.
- You can already see the back in here. By tapping on a card it turns around. By tapping on the "TurnCards" button several cards will show their text on the back.
- The easiest way to learn the index cards is to use the "quizmode".

[Quizmode]:
- You can evaluate the difficulty of your cards by swiping in different directions (left->easy, down->medium, right->hard, up->remain unaltered).
-In Quizmode you can turn the current shown card around aslo by tapping on it again.
-After turning around you will be provided more information about your vocabulary.
- If you like you can press the speakers symbol and the text will be read to you.
- The Wordnik API helps you by providing definitons and phrases that belong to the words on the backside.

Global:
- You can swipe left on someone else's sets (darkblue sets) and import it to your own collection of sets (in there it will appear as a copy with a lightblue style) Attention: Obviously you are not able to import your own sets.

Settings:
- On the top of the page are informations about your account displayed.
- Here you can change the language in which the text will be pronounced. (options are: German, English - GB, English - AM, Turkish, Italian, Spanish and French)
- You find the "Logout" Button also in here.
